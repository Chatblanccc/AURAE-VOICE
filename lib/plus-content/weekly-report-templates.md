# Weekly Report Templates

## Plus Detailed Report

Sections:

1. Weekly Summary
   - Total conversations
   - Total speaking minutes
   - Completion rate of daily plans
2. Skill Snapshot
   - Fluency trend
   - Accuracy trend
   - Pronunciation trend
   - Interaction trend
3. Top 3 Wins
4. Top 3 Fix Targets
5. Next Week Plan
   - 2 recommended scenarios
   - 1 focus behavior

Example opening:

"This week you completed 9 conversations and practiced for 126 minutes. Your fluency improved steadily, and your strongest gain was in turn-taking."

## Pro Advanced Report

Includes everything in Plus Detailed plus:

1. Deep Diagnostics
   - Frequent grammar error clusters
   - Pronunciation pattern clusters
2. Scenario Performance Matrix
   - Performance by category (travel/workplace/social/etc.)
3. Stretch Assignments
   - 3 advanced speaking tasks
4. Export-ready Summary
   - One-page progress brief for teacher/coach usage
